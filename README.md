### HR Attrition Analysis



##### Overview



This project analyzes employee attrition to identify key risk factors affecting workforce stability. The goal is to support HR teams with data-driven insights for improving retention and reducing hiring costs.



###### Objective



* Measure overall attrition and attrition rate



* Identify high-risk departments and job roles



* Analyze impact of salary, experience, and travel on attrition



* Support business decision-making with insights



###### Dataset



Employee-level HR dataset containing:

Age, Gender, Department, Job Role, Salary, Years at Company, Education, Business Travel, Attrition.



Tools Used



* Excel – Data cleaning and preparation



* SQL – Business analysis and querying



* Power BI – Dashboard (to be added)





###### Workflow



* Cleaned raw data in Excel



* Standardized columns and handled missing values



* Analyzed data using SQL



* Generated business insights





###### Key Insights



* Higher attrition in Sales and R\&D



* Lower salary groups show higher attrition



* Early-career employees leave more



* Frequent travel increases attrition risk





###### Business Value



-Helps HR teams reduce attrition, improve retention strategies, and make data-driven workforce decisions.
